## The Hybrid Scoring Engine

TypingPeak uses a dual-metric system to measure typing performance:

### 1. Standard WPM (Official Score)

Formula:

(Standard WPM) = (Correct Characters / 5) / Full Duration

Purpose:

- Measures consistency and stamina
- Uses full selected duration (30 / 60 / 120 seconds)
- Prevents inflated scores from early stopping

Key Principle:

Stopping early lowers your score — by design.

---

### 2. Live WPM (Real-Time Speed)

Formula:

(Live WPM) = (Correct Characters / 5) / Time Elapsed

Purpose:

- Displays burst typing speed
- Updates dynamically in real time
- Motivational feedback metric
